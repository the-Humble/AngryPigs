Angry Rabbits vs Foxes

Instructions

In order to play Angry Rabbits vs Foxes go to the menu and click the play button. You can also select a level in the main menu and it will be loaded so you can play it!
In the game screen, place your mouse in the point you wish the cannon ball hits and then click in it. The cannon ball will go there and you'll be able to see your points below the title.
Have fun!
